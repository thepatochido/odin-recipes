Recipe for bolognese spaghetti

Description

The recipe for spaghetti is very simple. The longest part is the bolognese sauce which i'ts all about the tomatoes, followed by the meat and finally the pasta itself.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo of red tomato
  • 400g of Red Tomato puree
  • 1/2 big white onion
  • 2 pieces of garlic
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1/2 of a bread's crumb
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/2 big white onion
  • 2 pieces of garlic
  • Spaghetti pasta

Steps (only for the bolognese sauce)

  1. Boil tomatos for 4 minutes and let them cool down
  2. Chop onion and garlic very fine
  3. Peel tomatoes and chop them finely
  4. Melt butter and olive oil in the pot
  5. Add chopped onion to the pot 4 minutes medium high heat
  6. Add garlic and oregano and stir for less than 1 minute
  7. Add chopped tomatoes
  8. Two minutes after add tomato puree and salt, stir well
  9. (Optional) Add 3 or 4 basil leaves
  10. Once it boils turn the heat down and let it boil for half an hour
