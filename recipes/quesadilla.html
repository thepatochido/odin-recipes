Quesadillas always have queso and that's a fact

How to make a proper quesadilla

Pretty easy recipe, just needs a little bit of caution with the pan

Ingredients

  • Queso oaxaca
  • Corn or flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Put the tortilla in a pan
  2. Adjust medium heat
  3. Add some oaxaca cheese stripes
  4. When the cheese starts to melt, fold the tortilla like closing a book
  5. Flip the tortilla as necessary for 3-4 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
